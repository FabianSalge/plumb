Plumb is installing. The pod reports ready once the pinned model weights are
loaded (first start downloads ~420 MB, so give it a few minutes).

Try the tracer bullet:

  kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "plumb.fullname" . }} 8000:{{ .Values.service.port }}

  curl -s localhost:8000/v1/verify -H 'content-type: application/json' -d '{
    "text": "The capital of France is Paris.",
    "context": ["Paris is the capital and largest city of France."],
    "mode": "fast"
  }'

Egress is default-deny except DNS and (while networkPolicy.allowModelDownload
is true) TCP 443 for the weight download. Set it false once weights are
mirrored in-cluster.
