# ADR-NNNN: Short imperative title

2026-MM-DD · Status: Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by ADR-NNNN

## Context

What situation forces a decision, and what constraints bound it. State the
options that were seriously considered and the evidence in hand (spikes,
benchmarks, issues) — enough that a reader can disagree with the decision
for the right reasons.

## Decision

What we will do, in one or two active sentences. The decision, not the
justification — that lives in Context.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, and what we are now committed to.
Include the honest costs; an ADR with no downsides wasn't needed.
